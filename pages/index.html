The problem

"I invited them… but who are they to me?"

Big families, distant cousins, friends-of-friends. At a 1,000-guest wedding, no host can remember every face — or how each guest connects to them. So greetings feel awkward, and real relationships go unrecognised.

How it works

Three simple steps

1

QR on every card

Each invitation carries a unique QR code. The guest scans it with any phone camera — no app to install.

2

Guest tells their relation

They enter who they are, how they're related to you, and which family members are coming along.

3

You see the whole network

Every guest appears in a live relationship flow diagram, grouped by family branch — instantly clear.
